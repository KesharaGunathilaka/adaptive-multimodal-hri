Based on the NTU data and HRI scenarios, I'm simplifying to 6 classes instead of 8. 
The reason: NTU has no clean "walking_toward" vs "walking_away" single-person action — that direction information will come from the z-velocity feature we pass to the fusion engine, not from the classifier label itself. 
Six clean, learnable classes:
0: sitting
1: standing
2: walking
3: running
4: stepping_back
5: slumped
